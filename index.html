Joining Moringa School

I have always been fascinated with programming ever since I interacted with it in my first year of campus. However, I never got the chance to proceed with it until I finished university and enrolled in a two-months bootcamp on HTML and CSS three years ago. The bootcamp was an eye-opener, triggering massive interest in the subject. I joined Moringa School for the software development program following recommendations from friends in the local IT field. Moreover, its partnership with renowned technology firms reinforces its leadership in training programming courses, making it the best choice to help with changing my career.
